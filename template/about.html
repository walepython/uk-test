About PassTheLifeInUKTest.co.uk

PassTheLifeInUKTest.co.uk was created to make preparing for the Life in the UK Test simpler, clearer, and more accessible for everyone.

We understand how important this test is for people applying for Indefinite Leave to Remain or British citizenship, and how stressful revision can feel without the right tools. That is why we built a free, easy-to-use practice platform focused on helping candidates revise effectively and gain confidence before test day.

Our practice questions are developed using the official Life in the UK: A Guide for New Residents (3rd Edition) handbook, which forms the basis of the current Life in the UK Test. The platform allows users to practise in different ways including by topic and through full mock-style tests so they can identify gaps in their knowledge and improve steadily.

We aim to keep this resource free, independent, and regularly updated, while making it practical for real exam preparation. The tests are designed to reflect the structure and difficulty of the official exam, helping users feel familiar with the format and timing.

PassTheLifeInUKTest.co.uk is an independent educational resource. We are not affiliated with the UK Home Office or any government body. Our goal is simply to support candidates by providing a helpful revision tool they can rely on.

We welcome feedback and suggestions from users, as this helps us continue improving the platform for future candidates.
